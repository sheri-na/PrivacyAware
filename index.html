What is Data Privacy?

Data Privacy is the protection of personal data from unauthorized access, ensuring individuals retain control over who can access their information.

About Us

With over half of the world's population now using the internet, safeguarding data privacy has become increasingly crucial.

Privacy Aware is a website designed with the everyday user in mind, aiming to empower individuals to better understand and assert their data privacy rights.Our platform equips users with the knowledge and tools necessary to navigate the complex landscape of online privacy, enabling them to take control of their personal data with confidence.
